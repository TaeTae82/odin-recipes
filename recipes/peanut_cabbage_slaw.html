Peanut Cabbage Slaw

Home Photo by Monsoon Spice

Description

A Thai inspired dish with little prep time.

    Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup Creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 6 cups shredded red cabbage
  • 1 bag 10 ounce frozen shelled edamame, thawed
  • 1 1/2 cups matchstick-cut carrots
  • 1 large English cucumber, cut into half moons
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup roasted salted peanuts
  • sesame seeds (optional)
    Steps
  1. Whisk peanut butter, rice vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, Sriracha, and ginger paste in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Place shredded cabbage, edamame, carrots, cucumber, and green onions in a large bowl. Toss to combine.
  3. Pour peanut butter dressing over salad. Stir to combine. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  4. Just before serving, top salad with peanuts and sesame seeds.

yields 12 servings
