Description

A quick and easy guacamole recipe

    Ingredients
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste
    Steps
  1. Mash avocados
  2. add onion, tomato, garlic
  3. season with lime juice, salt, pepper to taste
  4. cover and cool 30 minutes

yields 16 servings
